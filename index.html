About me

I’m a Mechatronics and Cyber-Physical Systems Engineer interested in turning complex technical ideas into working systems. My projects usually combine mechanical design, electronics, control, software, and user-centered interfaces.

I especially enjoy building robots and prototypes that solve practical problems, and I’m drawn to emerging technologies — mixed reality, edge AI and connected devices — exploring how they can make engineering more capable and more human.
